Introduction to Birds

Birds are a group of warm-blooded vertebrates constituting the class Aves, characterised by feathers, toothless beaked jaws, the laying of hard-shelled eggs, a high metabolic rate, a four-chambered heart, and a strong yet lightweight skeleton

Common Birds Species

There are numerous common types of birds found around the world. Here are a few examples of well-known bird species:

  1. American Robin (Turdus migratorius): Known for its reddish-orange breast, the American Robin is a migratory songbird found throughout North America.
  2. Hummingbird:There are various species of hummingbirds, known for their small size, iridescent feathers, and ability to hover in mid-air. They are found in the Americas.
  3. Sparrows:Sparrows are a diverse group of small passerine birds found globally. They come in various species and are known for their small size, chirping songs, and adaptability to urban environments.
